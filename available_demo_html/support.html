Login & Signup Issues
I forgot my password

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

I'm not receiving the verification email

Please check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, ensure you entered the correct email address. You can also try resending the verification email from the login screen after 2 minutes.

My account is locked

Accounts may be temporarily locked due to multiple failed login attempts. Please wait 30 minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact our support team using the form on this page.

How do I create a new account?

Click on "Register" or "Sign Up" on the homepage. Fill in your details (User ID, Email, Password, Referral ID if applicable) and click Submit. Verify your email to complete the process.

Account & General Support
How do I verify my identity (KYC)?

Go to Settings > Account Information. Upload the required documents (ID card, Passport, or Driver's License). Verification usually takes 24-48 hours.

How to withdraw my earnings?

Visit the Wallet page, click on Withdraw, select your method (Crypto - TRC20 or Stripe), enter the amount, and submit. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
