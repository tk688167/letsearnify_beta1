Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Let'$Earnify?

Let'$Earnify is a comprehensive hybrid platform that allows users to earn money through multiple channels including micro-tasks, freelancing services, diverse investment portfolios (Real Estate, Tech, SME), and a robust referral system.

Who can join the platform?

Our platform is open to anyone worldwide who is 18 years or older. Whether you are looking to earn active income through tasks or passive income through investments, you are welcome to join.

Earning & Investment

How does the Mudaraba Investment work?

Our Mudaraba Investment model is based on ethical profit-sharing. You deposit funds into specific pools (e.g., Real Estate), and our experts manage these investments. You receive a share of the profits generated, distributed according to the pre-agreed ratio.

How do I earn from referrals?

You earn a commission for every user who joins through your unique referral link and becomes an active member. We also offer multi-tier referral bonuses, meaning you earn when your referrals invite others.

Security & Support

Is my data safe?

Yes, we prioritize your security. We use banking-grade encryption to protect your personal information and transaction data. Your security is our top priority.

How can I contact support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach us via the Live Chat widget on the bottom right, email us at support@letsearnify.com, or reach out via WhatsApp.
