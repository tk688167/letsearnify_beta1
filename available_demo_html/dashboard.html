Frequently Asked Questions

How are pool rewards distributed?
Rewards are calculated weekly based on your tier and contribution level. Payouts are automated to your wallet.
Can I upgrade my royalty tier?
Yes, you can upgrade by increasing your total investment or referral count. Check the Royalty Pool section for criteria.
is the emergency fund refundable?
The emergency fund is a company reserve for operational safety. It is not directly withdrawable by users but ensures platform stability.
How long does withdrawal take?
Standard withdrawals take 1-3 business days. Priority withdrawals (Diamond Tier) are processed within 24 hours.
